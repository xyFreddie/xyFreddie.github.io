I started to learn playing tennis after I came to the Netherlands. I found this sport both challenging and fascinating. Handling the instant pressure on the court during competitions made me better in dealing with the stress I had in my daily life.

Technology

I got my technological kicks from extracurricular FIRST® LEGO® League competitions as early as middle school. I am self-driven by my interests in any techonology or domain knowledge that I might find useful or intriguing. For example building this website.

Culture

My favourite science fiction is Neuromancer by William Gibson. The narrative style appears to be chaotic at first like a cacophony of noise, and the tension throughout the plots seems unpleasant. Yet the intense touch of self-destruction and nihilism, desire and death, the ultimate rebellion after the disintegration of flesh and society norm captured and established the essence of the cyberpunk core.

As for fantasy I root for Wiedźmin (The Witcher series) by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski, (but definately not the Netflix version).

About Me

Freddie Liu (劉 辛瑜) has a solid technical background and a particular passion for data science. Beyond his professional life, He is a neurodiverse (ADD/ADHD) and he advocates for mental health awareness and diversity.

Freddie grew up in Shanghai. After obtained a bachelor of engineering in Computer Science, he decided to leave for the Netherlands to pursue a Master program focusing on Data Science and AI. The title image was taken by him right before he left near Jing'an Temple, an iconic and one of the most chic Soho areas in Shanghai, where he enjoyed great time with friends and family.
